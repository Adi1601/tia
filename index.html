The Intelligent Anamnesis

We develop smart digital anamnesis questionnaires that capture and visualize all relevant patient information so that doctors can quickly grasp the most important outcomes and better guide doctor-patient consultations. The Intelligent Anamnesis is a collaborative project between TUM Venture Labs and the TUM Sports Orthopedics department.

  • Learn More

Benefits

  • Complete Information

    The Intelligent Anamnesis is designed to secure all relevant facts and ensure no piece of information is overlooked, forgotten or lost in translation.

  • Dynamic questionnaire

    The questionnaire is designed to only request information that is relevant to the doctor’s diagnosis process - saving time for both doctors and patients.

  • Visualized outcomes

    All data is displayed in a doctor-friendly way enabling doctors to quickly retrieve the relevant information and save time in the doctor-patient consultation by being able to guide the patient through the most important talking points.

  • Smart recommendations

    The Intelligent Anamnesis provides doctors with recommendations for specific tests and examinations that can help exclude or confirm a list of possible diagnosis.
